Calling all innovators and storytellers

We're greater than the sum of our parts

We take the culture we’ve created very seriously. Our ability to collaborate, communicate and create is built on a foundation of levity, of enjoyment and balance between work and play. Having said that, we’re not in the mobile industry to play games. And we never will be. As we see it, going all-in on a 99-cent mashup of candy bar themed Tetris and Solitaire isn’t a road we’re interested in taking. We believe it’s bigger than that. We want to build products that change people’s lives; experiences that create connections between users and brands that transcend mere entertainment value and affect the very core of the way they see the world. Innovation, collaboration, and fun are at the core of everything we hold near and dear at YML. If you want to elevate your career while having a great time, then we want to talk to you.
